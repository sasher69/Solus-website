About the product

Engineered
for endurance.

SOLUS CARB MIX is built on one principle: the science of carbohydrate fuelling settled years ago, and most sports nutrition brands ignored it. The 1:0.8 maltodextrin-to-fructose ratio activates two separate intestinal transporters simultaneously — SGLT1 and GLUT5 — allowing the body to absorb up to 90 grams of carbohydrate per hour. That's 50% more than single-source glucose products.

We made it unflavoured as the default because we believe your gut should determine what you drink, not your palate. No artificial sweeteners, no colours, no caffeine, no amino acids you don't need mid-race. Just clean, fast-absorbing fuel that works in a bottle, a bidon, or mixed into a homemade gel.

For athletes who want flavour, we've added naturally-derived options using real fruit extracts at concentrations that won't cause gastric upset at high doses. The formula doesn't change. Only the flavour does.

Getting started

How to use.

  1. 01

    Mix 1 scoop (50g) with 500–750ml of water. Shake for 15 seconds until dissolved.

  2. 02

    Consume 60–90g of carbohydrates per hour during exercise — 1 to 2 scoops depending on intensity and duration.

  3. 03

    Best used during sessions over 90 minutes. Below that threshold, water and a pre-session meal is typically sufficient.

  4. 04

    Store in a cool, dry place after opening. Reseal lid tightly. Best consumed within 3 months of opening.
